# Spice Trade
Spice Trade is a text-based economic simulator and RPG. You play as a traveling merchant in a world where wealth is measured not just in numbers, but in the weight and purity of the coins in your purse.

## The Concept
Inspired by the realism of Spice and Wolf and the procedural depth of Dwarf Fortress, this project focuses on:

- Complex Economy: Prices driven by regional supply, demand, and seasons.
- Realistic Currency: Multiple mints, varying metal purity, and coin debasement.
- Logistics: Managing cargo, travel time, and market information.
- CLI Purity: A focused, data-driven experience powered by .NET and Spectre.Console.

## Roadmap
The project is currently in its infancy. Development is focused on building a robust simulation core:

- [ ] Core Engine: Implementation of the base economic models and currency logic.
- [ ] Trading Systems: Basic buy/sell mechanics and inventory management.
- [ ] The World: Travel systems between settlements and time progression.
- [ ] Dynamic Markets: Implementation of price fluctuations and regional modifiers.
- [ ] Advanced Simulation: Procedural generation, NPC merchants, and rumor systems.

Development is ongoing and the path forward expands with every milestone.
